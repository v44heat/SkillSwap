Why SkillSwap

Everything you need to learn and teach

A structured platform built for campus communities — making peer-to-peer learning seamless, safe, and rewarding.

Discover skills easily

Search and filter skills offered by fellow students. Filter by subject, department, or availability to find exactly what you need.

Book sessions instantly

Send session requests and schedule learning meetings with a few clicks. Both parties get notified and can confirm availability.

Build your reputation

Give and receive ratings after each session. Build a trusted profile that showcases your teaching ability and reliability.

Smart messaging

Communicate with potential exchange partners directly within the platform before committing to a session.

Safe campus community

All users are verified campus students. Admin oversight ensures the platform remains trustworthy and spam-free.

Track your progress

View your session history, skills learned, skills taught, and performance metrics — all in one place.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

Four simple steps to start exchanging skills with your campus community.

1

Create your profile

Sign up with your campus email and build a profile that showcases who you are and what you study.

2

List your skills

Add the subjects or skills you're confident teaching. Set your availability and preferred session format.

3

Find or request

Browse available skills, find the right match, and send a session request to begin the exchange.

4

Learn and rate

Complete your session, then leave an honest rating to help the community grow stronger together.
